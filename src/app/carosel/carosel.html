A$AP ROCKY × PUMA

PUMA has announced a groundbreaking partnership with A$AP Rocky as the new Creative Director for the F1 category. Known for his trailblazing style and cultural influence, A$AP Rocky brings fresh perspective and creative energy to the PUMA brand.

This collaboration is not just about fashion—it’s a fusion of motorsport, streetwear, and innovation. Fans can expect limited edition drops, immersive campaigns, and bold designs that redefine performance lifestyle.

A$AP Rocky said, “Working with PUMA allows me to bring my ideas to life and push the boundaries of style in sport.”

Puma × Portugal Collaboration

Portugal, the 2016 European Champions and 2018/19 Nations League Winners, are known for their captivating style of play.

“Signing a top-tier national team was one of our priorities in football,” said Arne Freundt, PUMA CEO.

For PUMA, the deal offers the opportunity to continue writing Portuguese football history.

“We were attracted to PUMA because of the way they engage with fans,” said Fernando Gomes, President of the FPF.

PUMA will unveil new products and creative direction for the partnership in early 2025.
